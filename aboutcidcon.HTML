Project Planing And Feasibility Studies

Project planning and feasibility studies involve evaluating the viability and potential success of a project before it begins. This includes assessing factors like market demand, technical feasibility, financial viability, and potential risks. The goal is to determine if the project is achievable, sustainable, and worth pursuing. Planning outlines the steps, timeline, and resources needed to execute the project effectively

Program Evalation Studies

Program evaluation studies assess the effectiveness, efficiency, and impact of programs or interventions.
